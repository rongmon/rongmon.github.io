Congratulations to graduate student Simon Wu for being selected as an NC Space Grant Graduate Research Fellow for the 2026–2027 academic year!

The fellowship will support Simon's research mapping the complex, small-scale structure of the circumgalactic medium. This work is critical for understanding how gas flows in and out of galaxies and shapes their evolution.
